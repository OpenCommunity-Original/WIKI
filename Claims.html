üìù Guide

  1. üè∞ Claim Creation
    Find the desired location and type:
    /c claim
    Now let's create a powercell. Place a chest and arrange the crafting materials inside.
    You can view the crafting recipe using the command:
    /c recipe
    The powercell should be placed in the same chunk where the command was executed.
    The powercell allows you to manage settlement settings, track members, and their activity.
  2. üè∑Ô∏è Claim Renaming
    A claim initially has the same name as the leader's nickname.
    To change it, use the command:
    /c name
  3. üîç Claim Expansion
    Expanding the claim can be done in several ways: one chunk at a time, with a radius to form a square, with a radius to form a circle.
    Respectively:
    /c claim
    /c square <radius>
    /c round <radius>
  4. üë• Adding Members
    To invite a member, write the command:
    /c invite <player>
    And the member, in turn, can accept the invitation using:
    /c accept
  5. ‚ö° Powercell Supply
    The powercell requires resources to function.
    These resources can include: iron, gold, emeralds, diamonds, coins.
    Each resource adds a certain amount of time.
    If the resources run out, the claim will disband within a couple of days.

üìú Commands

  • /c reload - Reload the Configuration and Language files.
  • /c claim - Claim the land you are currently standing in for your claim.
  • /c unclaim - Unclaim land from your claim.
  • /c show - Visualize claims around you.
  • /c invite <player> - Invite a player to join your claim.
  • /c accept - Accept the latest claim invitation.
  • /c addmember <player> - Add a player to access your claim.
  • /c kick <member> - Kick a member from your claim.
  • /c dissolve - Dissolve your claim.
  • /c leave <claim> - Leave a claim that you are a member of.
  • /c lock - Lock or unlock your claim.
  • /c home <claim> - Go to a claim's home.
  • /c sethome - Set the home for your claim.
  • /c ban <member> - Ban a member from your claim.
  • /c unban <member> - Unban a member from your claim.
  • /c recipe - View the recipe for a powercell.
  • /c setspawn - Set the spawn point for various uses such as ejecting and banning.
  • /c name <name> - Set the display name for your claim.
  • /c setowner <new_owner> - Transfer the right to manage the claim.
  • /c square <radius> - Expand the claim along the radius (square).
  • /c round <radius> - Expand the claim along the radius (circle).

    • Moderator Commands:

  • /c admin removeclaim - Remove a claim you're standing in.
  • /c admin transferownership <player> - Transfer the claim you are standing in to another player.
  • /c admin name <name> - Set the display name for the claim you are standing in.
  • /c admin lock - Lock or unlock the claim you are standing in.
